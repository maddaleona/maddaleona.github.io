Me in a Nutshell

I love anything creative and inspiring.
I would spend my life traveling (the more distant and cold the place is, the better), perhaps capturing some unsuspecting landscape – and always listening to music. Photography occupies a big part of my heart as well – all photos here are mine.

I love food, books, and movies (who doesn't?), and I really enjoy talking about them with equally passionate people. Dogs – which I always politely greet when I meet them – make me feel like a child again.
