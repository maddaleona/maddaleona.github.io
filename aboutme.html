Me in a Nutshell

I love anything creative and inspiring.

I'd spend my life traveling, seeking the most remote, cold places and their unexpected landscapes. – always with music in my ears.

Or the latest episode of "Indagini" by Stefano Nazzi.

Photography occupies a big part of my heart as well – all photos here are mine.

I love food, books, and movies (who doesn't?), and I really enjoy talking about them with equally passionate people.

Dogs – which I always politely greet when I meet them – make me feel like a child again.
